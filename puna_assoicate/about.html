About Us

The Poona Electric & Electronics Association was established to serve as the unified voice of the electrical industry in the Pune region. We represent a diverse community of traders, electricians, contractors, suppliers, educators, and industry leaders.

Our mission is to advance the electrical trade by promoting safety, technical excellence, innovation, and workforce development.

Founded on the principles of integrity, collaboration, and service, we are committed to advocating for the interests of our members. We believe a strong, skilled electrical workforce is vital to powering homes, businesses, and the future. Through strategic partnerships, policy engagement, and continuous education, EMA works to drive the growth, sustainability, and success of the industry.

Whether you're an apprentice, an experienced electrician, a business owner, or an industry stakeholder — we’re here to support, connect, and lead.
