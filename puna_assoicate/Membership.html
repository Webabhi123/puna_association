Why PEEA Membership

  • Representation:

    PEEA identifies and brings together likeminded to represent the voice of Traders, Manufacturers, Contractors etc.

  • Active Advocacy:

    We promote active representation in the Government as well as institutions and industry/trade organisations in India and abroad.

  • Problem Solving:

    PEEA represents to the government, problems faced by its members, individually or as a group, to find solutions and provide relief from unreasonable acts of administrative and policy making departments.

  • Professional Services:

    PEEA provides a bouquet of professional and value-added services to its members.

  • Upgraded Services:

    ELCINA services are tailored to serve the best interest of its Members & community to empower it to face competition and prosper. These services are constantly upgraded to suit the changing demands of the environment and needs of the industry.

  • Digital Platform:

    PEEA's E-commerce facilities and its dynamic website together attract about many visitors per day and provide a platform for members.

  • Information & Research:

    PEEA is a repository of information and research, readily available for its members and helps them in taking critical decisions based on relevant facts.

  • Inclusive Membership:

    The Executive Committee may choose to admit as a member an organization which does not meet the above criteria, if it feels the same would support the cause and add value for its members.

Membership Eligibility & Types

  • Eligibility:

    A Company or a firm engaged or interested in electrical trade shall be eligible to become a Member of Association, provided they have ONE YEAR standing in the business. The Applicant should provide the proof of GST Registration Number & application must be approved by the Managing Committee.

  • Life Members:

    A Company or a Firm can become a Life Member by paying fees, only once. The Life Member shall have the right of voting similar to Annual Members.

  • Annual Members:

    A Company or a firm can become an Annual Member by paying in advance, an Annual Subscription Fee together with one time Admission Fee.

  • Application Process:

    The application form, as prescribed by the Association, shall contain the name, address and nature of business of the applicant together with name and address of Proprietor / Partners and Directors and other relevant information. All applications for membership shall be accompanied by the full amount of Donations / Fees / annual subscription fees, as applicable.

  • Outstation Members:

    Any Firm or Company engaged in Electrical Trade whose business address is out of the area mentioned in clause can become a member of the Association with following Terms and Conditions:

    • Application form of outstation Member as prescribed by the Association shall fulfill the conditions as per clause (C) & (D) above.
    • Applicant must be registered member of its local association & copy of its membership certificate along with the recommendation letter from its representative association should accompany the application form.
    • Outstation member will not have any Voting Right nor be allowed to be Member of the Managing Committee.

  • Committee Discretion:

    The Managing Committee shall have full discretion and power to decide whether an applicant possesses the requisite qualifications to become a member. The Committee shall not be bound to assign any reason for rejecting any application and its decision shall be final.

Subscription Fees

  • Life Members: Rs. 10,000/- only once (Plus GST as applicable)
  • Annual Members: Rs. 2,500/- Plus Admission Fee Rs. 1,000/- only once (Plus GST as applicable)

The Annual Subscription shall fall due in the Month of April every year.
